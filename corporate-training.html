REGULATORY FRAMEWORK ALIGNMENT

All corporate programmes align to international standards and regulatory requirements, ensuring immediate practical application

NIST CSF 2.0

NIST Cybersecurity Framework

Updated framework covering Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover, and the new Govern function. Essential for US market compliance and international best practice.

  • • Strategic risk management and governance structures
  • • Implementation roadmaps and maturity assessments
  • • Board-level reporting and KPI frameworks
NCSC CAF

NCSC Cyber Assessment Framework

UK national standard for cyber security assessment, providing systematic approach to understanding and improving cyber security across organisations.

  • • 14 principles across 4 strategic objectives
  • • Self-assessment tools and capability mapping
  • • Integration with supply chain security requirements
DORA

Digital Operational Resilience Act

EU regulation mandating operational resilience for financial services, with January 2025 implementation deadline. Critical for financial institutions and their technology partners.

  • • ICT risk management and third-party oversight
  • • Incident reporting and digital operational resilience testing
  • • Threat-led penetration testing requirements
ISO 27001:2022

ISO 27001 Information Security

International standard for information security management systems, providing systematic approach to managing sensitive company information and maintaining security.

  • • Information security management system implementation
  • • Risk assessment methodologies and control frameworks
  • • Certification readiness and audit preparation
