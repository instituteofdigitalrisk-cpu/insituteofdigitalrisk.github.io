FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about enrollment, programmes, and career outcomes

What are the admission requirements?

Undergraduate degree or equivalent professional experience, demonstrated interest in cybersecurity, and commitment to intensive learning. Technical background helpful but not required for most programmes.

Can I study part-time while working?

The 6-month Cyber Resilience Programme offers evening and weekend options for working professionals. Executive programmes are intensive 2-week formats designed for minimal career disruption.

What certification do I receive upon completion?

IDR Professional Certificate plus preparation for industry certifications including CISSP, CISM, and framework-specific credentials. Academic credit transfer available with partner institutions.

What is the job placement rate?

92% of graduates achieve career progression within 12 months. Dedicated career services team provides CV optimization, interview preparation, and direct connections to our corporate partner network.

Is international enrollment available?

Yes, we welcome international students. Hybrid delivery model includes online components for global accessibility. Visa guidance provided for in-person programme components in London.

What technology and equipment do I need?

Laptop meeting minimum specifications provided in acceptance package. Access to enterprise-grade security tools and lab environments included in programme fee. No additional software purchases required.
